French Toast Casserole

Description

French Toast Casserole is a warm and comforting breakfast dish made with chunks of bread soaked in a sweet, spiced custard and baked until golden.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of bread, cut into cubes
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Butter for greasing
  • Powdered sugar for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease a baking dish with butter.
  3. Spread the bread cubes evenly in the dish.
  4. Whisk eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla.
  5. Pour the mixture over the bread.
  6. Bake for 40-50 minutes until set and golden.
  7. Serve warm with powdered sugar on top.
