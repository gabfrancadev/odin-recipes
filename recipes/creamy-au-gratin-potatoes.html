Creamy au Gratin Potatoes

Description

Creamy au Gratin Potatoes are a classic comfort food made with thinly sliced potatoes layered in a rich, creamy sauce and baked until tender and golden.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated cheese (cheddar or gruyère)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for greasing

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease a baking dish with butter.
  3. Layer the sliced potatoes evenly in the dish.
  4. Mix cream, garlic, salt, and pepper, then pour over the potatoes.
  5. Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 45-60 minutes until tender and golden.
  7. Let rest for a few minutes before serving.
